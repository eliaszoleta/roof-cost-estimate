Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a roof replacement cost?

Roof replacement typically costs $5,000–$15,000 for an average home, depending on size, material, and location. Asphalt shingles average $3.50–$5.50 per sq ft installed.

How much does roof repair cost?

Minor roof repairs cost $150–$400. Major repairs like replacing large sections or fixing structural damage can run $1,000–$3,500 depending on the scope.

How much does a metal roof cost?

Metal roofing costs $7,000–$25,000 installed for an average home. Standing seam metal runs $10–$17 per sq ft while corrugated metal is $3.50–$6.50 per sq ft.

How long does a roof last?

Asphalt shingles last 20–30 years. Metal roofs last 40–70 years. Tile and slate roofs can last 50–100 years with proper maintenance.
